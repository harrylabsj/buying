# Sources

Use this file when the user wants research sources or when live price validation is needed.

## General Rule

Prefer:
- sold prices over asking prices
- official sources over rumor sources
- recent market evidence over stale "MSRP" anchors

## Electronics

Good sources:
- official stores
- major marketplaces with clear seller identity
- recent sold listings
- price tracking tools or cached price history
- reputable review or comparison sites

## Vehicles

Good sources:
- recent sold comps
- VIN and history services
- dealer listings with transparent fees
- local market comps

## Real Estate

Good sources:
- recent sold comps
- listing history
- local tax and transaction records
- neighborhood market summaries

## Services

Good sources:
- competitor quotes
- official pricing pages
- plan comparison pages
- customer retention offers when available

## B2B And SaaS

Good sources:
- pricing pages
- public procurement or user reports
- product comparison sites
- current contract terms from the user

## Groceries And Daily Essentials

Good sources:
- grocery platforms
- supermarket apps
- instant retail listings
- warehouse clubs
- local store flyers when current

## Subscriptions

Good inputs:
- user-provided list of subscriptions
- monthly or annual cost
- last usage date
- whether it overlaps another tool

If the skill needs subscription data, the user should provide it or explicitly let the agent inspect it through an approved connector.
