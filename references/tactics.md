# Tactics

Use this file when the user wants help negotiating, cancelling, or getting a better retention offer.

## Negotiation Basics

Always define:
- target price
- walk-away price
- strongest evidence

Keep scripts short and specific.

## Retail Match Script

Use when a competitor price is credible.

> I found the same item for X at a comparable seller. Can you match it or offer a similar total price after shipping and fees?

## Used Goods Script

Use when sold comps support a lower price.

> Similar items have recently sold around X. If you can do that range, I'm ready to move quickly.

## Bundle Script

Use when the seller may not cut price but can improve total value.

> If price is firm, can you include shipping, accessories, installation, or an extended return window?

## Retention Script For Bills

Use for internet, insurance, utilities, or subscription renewals.

> I've been a customer for X time and I'm reviewing alternatives. What can you do to keep me at a better rate?

## Cancellation Script

Use for subscriptions the user wants to cut.

> I haven't used this enough to justify the current price. Before I cancel, is there a lower-cost or pause option?

## Negotiation Guardrails

Do:
- use real market evidence
- stay polite and direct
- ask for total value, not only list price

Do not:
- bluff with fake comps
- keep negotiating after your walk-away point
- move off-platform without a strong reason and clear protection
